About the Book

From the void emerges the USS Azar — a relic of an unknown Cataclysm that wiped out many of humanity's interstellar colonies and disconnected them from Earth. Its timely arrival brings a glimmer of hope to Dr. Mayakannan and her crew aboard Xerxes Station after the loss of a fifteen-year-old psychic, Vega Santos.

Mayakannan secretly launches the Azar Project in direct defiance of ExoLink, the largest conglomerate in the Hadrian Quadrant, currently holding her by the neck with a dubious contract that stands in the way of her mission to uncover the secrets of the past and prevent another catastrophe.

Ill omen casts a shadow over her team. The mysterious preternatural illness that took Vega threatens others in her circle. Grief drives a wedge in the cracks of her team's already-fragile relationships — namely with Quiz, psychic former corporate intelligence goon for ExoLink, and Silvestre, Xerxes Station's head custodian and Vega's father.

The Azar Project is a chance for humanity's future, but only if it doesn't tear them apart first.

◆ Vega

Vega Santos

Psychic — Xerxes Station

Fifteen years old. Gone before the story begins. Her loss sets everything in motion — and the mysterious illness that took her hasn't finished with Xerxes Station yet.

The Setting

Xerxes Station

Perched at the edge of the Hadrian Quadrant, Xerxes Station is one of humanity's remaining outposts following the Cataclysm — the unknown event that severed Earth's connection to its interstellar colonies and destroyed many of them entirely. Under ExoLink's looming corporate influence, the station's small crew works to piece together what happened and what it means for the colonies that are left.

Then the USS Azar emerges from the void. And nothing is simple anymore.
