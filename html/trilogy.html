01 Book One The Azar Project

From the void emerges the USS Azar, a relic of an unknown Cataclysm that wiped out many of humanity's interstellar colonies, disconnecting them from Earth. Its timely arrival brings a glimmer of hope to Dr. Mayakannan and her crew aboard Xerxes Station after the loss of fifteen-year-old psychic, Vega Santos.

Ill omen casts a shadow over her team. The preternatural illness that took Vega threatens others in her circle as ExoLink, the largest conglomerate in the quadrant, watches their every move.

Mayakannan secretly launches the Azar Project in direct defiance of ExoLink. Their dubious contract stands in the way of her mission to uncover the mysteries of the past and prevent another catastrophe, but the project must go on.

Standing at her side is former ExoLink-goon, Quiz, and Vega's custodian father, Silvestre. Grief drives a wedge in the cracks of her team's already-fragile relationships. Buried secrets, dark pasts and messy family ties will come to a head.

A chance for humanity's future, but only if it doesn't tear them apart first.
